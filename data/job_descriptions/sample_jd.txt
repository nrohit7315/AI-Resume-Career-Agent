Job Title: AI/ML Engineer (Entry to Mid-Level)

About the Role:
We are looking for an AI/ML Engineer to join our product engineering team. You will
build and deploy machine learning and NLP systems that power real product features,
working closely with data and backend teams.

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, or a related field.
- 2+ years of experience in machine learning, deep learning, or NLP.
- Strong proficiency in Python and hands-on experience with scikit-learn, TensorFlow
  or PyTorch.
- Experience with NLP techniques such as TF-IDF, embeddings, or transformer models
  (BERT, GPT).
- Familiarity with pandas, numpy for data processing and feature engineering.
- Experience building and consuming REST APIs (FastAPI or Flask).
- Working knowledge of Git and collaborative development workflows.
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in a team.

Nice to Have:
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
- Exposure to MLOps practices and model deployment.
- Familiarity with SQL and data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI).

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement NLP-based similarity and ranking systems.
- Build data pipelines to parse and process unstructured text data.
- Train, evaluate, and deploy machine learning models to production.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate business requirements
  into ML solutions.
